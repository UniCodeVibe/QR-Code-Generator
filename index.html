How to Create a Free QR Code for Printing

If you are printing flyers, business cards, or billboards, you need a code that scales without blurring. Most generators force you to pay for high-quality formats. We provide them for free.

  1. Enter your link: Paste your website URL, WiFi password, or vCard details into the input box.
  2. Customize Colors: Ensure there is high contrast. Dark foreground on light background works best for scanners.
  3. Select SVG: For professional printing, click Download Vector SVG. This format allows your designer to resize the code to any size without losing quality.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do static QR codes expire?

No. Static QR codes encode the data directly into the image pattern. As long as your website URL is active, the QR code will work forever.

Can I use this for commercial use?

Yes. The codes generated here are 100% free for personal and commercial projects. There are no watermarks.
